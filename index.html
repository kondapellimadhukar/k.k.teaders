AgroAssist – AI-Assisted Crop Diagnosis and Fertilizer Guidance

Farmers often face crop leaf problems such as yellow leaves, leaf spots, pest attacks, and nutrient deficiencies. Many farmers are unsure about which fertilizer or treatment is suitable. AgroAssist helps farmers upload crop images, select symptoms, and receive AI-assisted preliminary diagnosis, precautions, and fertilizer suggestions, which can be verified by K.K TRADERS.
